The Tournament

Everything You Need to Know

An annual gathering of competitors, friends, and golf enthusiasts for a day of elite competition.

2-Man Scramble

Both players tee off, select the best shot, and play from that spot. Pure team strategy and execution from the first tee to the 18th hole.

Multiple Awards

Compete for the overall championship title, plus special honours for Longest Drive and Closest to the Pin on select holes.

Photo Challenges

Selected holes feature mandatory photo challenges. Capture the moment before entering your score — creating memories that last.

Live Scoreboard

Spectators and supporters can follow along in real time as scores update throughout the day on our live leaderboard.

Annual Tradition

Held once a year, the Lorgen Invitational brings together the best teams for an unforgettable day of golf and camaraderie.

Hall of Fame

Past champions are immortalised in our Legacy section — a permanent record of those who have risen to claim the Lorgen title.
