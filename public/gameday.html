How to Play

2-Man Scramble Format

A team format designed for maximum fun and competition

1

Both Players Tee Off

Both team members hit their tee shots. Select the best drive — that's where play continues from.

2

Play from Best Ball

Both players pick up and play from the chosen spot. Repeat this process for every shot until holed out.

3

Record the Score

Once the ball is holed, record the total number of strokes taken. Enter it via the app after completing the hole.

4

Photo Challenges

On designated holes, you must upload a photo before your score is accepted. Follow the app's prompt — it's part of the fun!
